Hey there! I'm Yurii an AI/ML Engineer who believes the best way to learn is by building. After spending years developing production ML systems and teaching at MIPT, I'm now channeling that experience into something bigger: YuriODev.

I recently wrapped up the intensive MLX Applied Machine Learning program, where I dove deep into the cutting edge Transformers, RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) systems, PEFT/LoRA fine-tuning, Vision Transformers, and the art of deploying it all with Docker/Kubernetes. But what excites me most? Taking these complex technologies and making them accessible to everyone.

My journey's been quite the adventure: from modeling chaos theory at 14 and attending CERN's summer program, to building ML systems that detect sleep patterns with 87% accuracy and predict property values across the UK. Along the way, I've published research that transformed how educational institutions approach grading automation.

Now, I'm building YuriODev because I believe AI education needs a revolution. No more dry theory or copy-paste tutorials. Instead: interactive coding environments, real-world projects, and a path from "Hello World" to deploying your own transformer models. It's the platform I wish existed when I started and I'm making it happen.

Whether you're curious about AI, looking to level up your ML skills, or interested in collaborating on the future of tech education let's connect and build something awesome together!
